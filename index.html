Placements

The Department of Placement and Career Guidance shoulders the responsibility of training the students and placing them in esteemed companies. Students from all the year and branches are trained for placement systematically to achieve the desired results.

The department meticulously works in networking with the companies and HRs of varied fields and schedules campus drives accordingly.

The department with gusto strives to cater and hone the students’ technical, communication and interview skills.

To become the benchmark institution in Placement, Entrepreneur Development and developing the students for Higher Education by constantly monitoring the dynamic changes in the requirements of various fields and accordingly address the needs of the students by training and updating them.
